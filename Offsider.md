  Privacy Policy for Offsider
  Last updated: May 2026

  Offsider does not collect, store, or share any personal data on its own servers.

  DATA STORAGE
  All list data is stored locally on your device and, if you are signed into iCloud,
  synced to your private iCloud account via Apple CloudKit. This data is only
  accessible to you and to Apple under their privacy policy (apple.com/legal/privacy).

  LIST SHARING
  If you use the sharing feature, a CloudKit share link is created. Anyone with
  that link can view and edit the shared list. You control who receives the link.

  PURCHASES
  In-app purchases are handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store any
  payment information.

  ANALYTICS & TRACKING
  Offsider does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

  CONTACT
  For privacy questions: offsider.lists@gmail.com
